The Best Movies According to Diin

My top 3 movies of all time.

2001: A Space Odyssey

Best Kubrick film. Love the aesthetic, the message, the tone and the significance it had.

Blade Runner 2049

One of the most visually appealing movies ever. Kept me engaged and enthralled throughout as I began to question what it means to be human.

Memento

A very uniquelly made movie, the story is told in a completely counter-intuitive way. Just as it intended to.
